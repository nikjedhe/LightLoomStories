You + your girls

Why I pick up the camera

I'm a dad of two daughters. I know how fast it goes.

One day you're holding a newborn who fits along your forearm. Then you blink, and she's correcting your pronunciation and asking to borrow your headphones. Nobody warns you that "they grow up so fast" is not a cliché — it's a deadline.

That's why I photograph families. Not the stiff, everyone-say-cheese kind of photos — the real ones. The way your toddler laughs with her whole body. The way grandpa watches the birthday candles. The way you look at your people when you think no one's watching.

Time flies. Let me help you keep the good parts.

— Nikhil, Lightloom Stories

How it works

Simple, relaxed, kid-approved.

Say hello

Send me a message on Instagram or email. Tell me about your family, the occasion, or just that "the kids are growing too fast." I get it.

The session

We pick a spot and a time with good light. No stiff posing — we play, we wander, we let the kids be kids. The best photos happen when everyone forgets the camera is there.

Your gallery

Within days, you get a private online gallery — beautifully edited, ready to view, share, and download in full resolution. Yours to keep, forever.
